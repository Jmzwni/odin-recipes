Egyptian Beans

Description

This dish is a simple one, made of the Egyptian beans, and a few other ingredients. The steps of preparation is basically cooking the beans, the finish dish is made of the following:

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Oil
  • Green Vegetables
  • Salt
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cook the beans in a pot of only water until it becomes soft
  2. Take it out of fire, and add the following on serving
  3. Put appropriate quantity of the beans on plate for serving
  4. Add oil as appropriate, and some sliced pieces Onion
  5. Add salt and the green Vegetables, chopped
  6. Finally, serve with bread
